Neuro Vision


“Neuro Vision” is a project that aim to create a wristband that enables students visualize and further store the amazing ideas inside their head.
We went through steps like Empathy, Ideation, Prototype, Modeling, Filming and Editing to finalize the work.
I personally contributed most in the Ideation and Filming process and support other members in other stages during the project.
