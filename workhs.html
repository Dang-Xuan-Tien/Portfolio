Highschool’s Activities

This video/short film was made when I was in grade 11th. It was the first time I had the chance to participate in such project with my team called “Ban Mai News”
In this video, we captured the happy life of students in Ban Mai secondary school, and how the teachers and workers feel about the time working there to celebrate the school’s 10th birthday.
I was the one who writes the content for the script and works with everyone to film and edit.

This video was made to be presented on screen in the graduation ceremony for the 9th and 12th graders.
The ceremony followed the concept of memory station, divided into 5 different stations namely: Memory, Youth, Regrets, Happiness, and Maturity.
I came up with ideas for each station, shoot videos, and edit to produce the final result.
